About

Integrative Practice for
Transformational Change

I am Nancy Robertson — Senior Coach, Psychotherapist, and Counsellor with over 15 years of experience in transformational work. My practice bridges the depth of psychotherapy, the direction of coaching, and the compassion of counselling.

As a Senior Accredited BACP Psychotherapist, I offer deep clinical insight into trauma, anxiety, and relationship patterns. As a Coach trained with Steve Chandler at ACS Coaching School, I help high-performers and industry leaders break through limitations and achieve extraordinary results. As a Counsellor, I provide a safe, holding space for authentic healing.

This unique integration allows me to meet you exactly where you are — whether you need therapeutic healing, strategic transformation, or compassionate support.
