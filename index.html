About Me

Nancy Robertson

Welcome. I'm Nancy Robertson (formerly Nancy Florence), a registered psychotherapist and counsellor dedicated to supporting individuals through their unique life journeys.

With years of experience in therapeutic practice, I provide a confidential, non-judgmental space where you can explore your thoughts, feelings, and experiences. My approach is warm, professional, and tailored to your individual needs.

I believe in the transformative power of therapy and the resilience of the human spirit. Whether you're facing a specific challenge or seeking deeper self-understanding, I'm here to walk alongside you.

My Approach

Integrative Therapy

I practice as an integrative psychotherapist, which means I draw from various therapeutic approaches to create a personalized treatment plan that resonates with your unique needs and circumstances.

01

Person-Centred

Creating a warm, accepting environment where you feel truly heard and understood

02

Psychodynamic

Exploring how past experiences shape present patterns and relationships

03

Cognitive-Behavioural

Practical strategies for changing unhelpful thought patterns and behaviours
